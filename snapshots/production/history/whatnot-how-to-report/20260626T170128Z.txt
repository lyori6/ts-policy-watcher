How to report by email
If you’re not sure where to report inappropriate behavior, email
trustandsafety@whatnot.com
and include:
The username of the person you’re reporting
A description of what happened
Helpful details, such as the show title (with date and time), product listing, photos, or screenshots
What happens after you report
Your report is confidential, so the person you report isn’t notified.
After you submit a report, our Trust & Safety team will:
Review the details you shared
Check relevant account history, including past shows
Gather and review evidence to determine whether our
Community Guidelines
were violated
Take
appropriate action
in line with our policies
During an investigation, we may temporarily restrict a user’s account. For example, we might suspend their ability to go live.
To keep Whatnot safe and fun, we also proactively investigate suspicious behavior and reports from the community. We regularly review seller and buyer activity to identify patterns or habitual policy abuse. When we detect a pattern supported by evidence, we take action.
Check the status of your report
You can check whether your report has been reviewed and see your report history directly on Whatnot.
For privacy reasons, we don’t share the specific outcome of individual investigations.
