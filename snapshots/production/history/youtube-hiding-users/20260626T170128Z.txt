Hide or unhide users from your channel
You can hide users and their associated accounts so that certain activity isn’t visible on your channel, including:
Comments
Live Chats
Community posts
Hidden users and their associated accounts also can’t
create clips from your videos and live streams
.
Keep in mind:
If you hide someone, they aren’t notified.
Past activity from associated accounts isn’t hidden.
Past comments from hidden users won’t show on your
Comments
page in YouTube Studio.
Hide a user
To hide someone from your channel:
Sign in to
YouTube
.
Find a comment that they’ve left on your channel or video.
Next to the comment, select More
Hide user from channel
.
Hide a user in YouTube Studio
To hide someone from your channel in YouTube Studio:
Find the user's
channel URL
from their channel homepage.
Go to
YouTube Studio
.
From the left menu, select
Settings
Community moderation
User management
.
Under the “Automated Filters” section, paste the channel URL in the “Hidden users” box.
Select
Save
.
Find users you’ve hidden
To view a list of users you’ve hidden:
Sign in to
YouTube Studio
.
From the left menu, select
Settings
Community
moderation
User management
.
Under the “Automated Filters” section, review users you’ve hidden in the “Hidden users” box.
Unhide a user
You can remove someone from your “Hidden users” list to show new comments they leave. To unhide a hidden user:
Sign in to
YouTube Studio
.
From the left menu, select
Settings
Community
moderation
User management
.
In the “Hidden users” box, find the user you'd like to show.
Select
Remove
next to their name.
Select
Save
.
